# Characters

This document records approved character and style decisions for generated project assets.

## Master Reference

Current master visual reference:

- `src/assets/images/scenarios/shoes/shot-001-establishing-hallway.png`

Use this image as the visual anchor for subsequent generated illustrations until a newer master reference is explicitly approved.

## Child Character

- Age impression: around 3 years old.
- Face: soft round toddler face, gentle cheeks, small nose, natural child proportions.
- Hair: tousled medium-brown hair, soft loose strands, slightly messy morning look.
- Clothing: muted green long-sleeve top and soft blue pants.
- Shoes: small blue shoes with simple child-friendly shape.
- Emotional range: natural, subtle, emotionally readable but not exaggerated.
- Important constraint: expressions must remain open to multiple interpretations. Do not visually imply that the child is defiant, manipulative, guilty, or intentionally obstructive.

## Visual Style

- Modern children’s book illustration.
- Soft watercolor on warm textured paper.
- Warm morning light.
- Calm pastel palette: warm white, cream, light beige, light gray, muted green, soft blue, and restrained warm accents.
- Camera often at the child’s eye level to preserve empathy and emotional safety.
- Simple natural forms, expressive posture and gesture, no caricature.

## Generation Rules

For new illustrations based on the master reference, preserve:

- the same child;
- face proportions;
- hairstyle;
- clothing;
- color palette;
- watercolor-paper style;
- level of stylization.

Only change what the shot requires:

- emotion;
- pose;
- angle;
- composition;
- framing.

Do not include text, labels, UI elements, palette swatches, moodboard layouts, watermarks, or reference-sheet elements in generated app illustrations.
